About the course

Murdo Frazer Pitch & Putt

Run by husband-and-wife team, Murdo Frazer is a nine-hole course tucked into the trees off Pemberton Ave in North Vancouver. There's no tee time to book and no dress code to worry about — just walk up with your wedge and putter (or rent from the pro shop), and play a relaxed round surrounded by nature.
